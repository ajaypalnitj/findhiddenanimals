Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Find Hidden Animals. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our service:

  • Game progress and scores (stored locally in your browser)
  • Basic usage analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Improve our game experience
  • Analyze game usage patterns

Data Storage

Your game progress is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our game is used. This service may collect:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the game
  • Browser type
  • Device information

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain game functionality. These are required for saving your progress and settings.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@findhiddenanimals.com
