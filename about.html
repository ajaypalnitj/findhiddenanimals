About Find Hidden Animals

Find Hidden Animals is a unique browser-based game that combines sound, strategy, and education in an engaging way. Players use audio cues to locate invisible animals, making it both fun and accessible to players of all ages.

Our Mission

We aim to create engaging, educational games that:

  • Develop spatial awareness and listening skills
  • Teach interesting facts about different animals
  • Provide entertainment for all ages
  • Are accessible to everyone

How to Play

  1. Click around the game area to search for the hidden animal
  2. Listen to the bell sound - it gets louder as you get closer
  3. Find the animal to win and unlock new levels
  4. Learn interesting facts about each animal you find

Accessibility

Find Hidden Animals is designed to be accessible to players of all abilities. While the game uses sound as a primary mechanic, visual feedback is also provided to ensure everyone can enjoy the game.
